Free Bulk Sending Guide

Choose your phone type below

📱 Android — WhatsApp Business + vCard (100% Free)
No third-party apps needed. Works for unlimited contacts. Uses your existing WhatsApp.
1

Install WhatsApp Business from Play Store

Open Play Store → search WhatsApp Business → Install (free). Set it up with your dealer phone number. Takes 2 minutes.

2

Save your customers to phone contacts

In this portal → Send tab → Load contacts → tap "📇 Save Contacts to Phone (vCard)" → a .vcf file downloads → tap it → tap "Import all contacts".

All customers save as "AKAI D001 – Name" in your phone contacts.

3

Create a Broadcast List in WhatsApp Business

Open WhatsApp Business → tap ⋮ (3 dots top right) → tap New Broadcast → in search bar type AKAI D001 → all your customers appear → select all of them → tap the green tick → list is created.

Maximum 256 per list. If you have 500 contacts, create 2 lists. 1000 contacts = 4 lists.

4

Send image to everyone — 1 tap per list

Open WhatsApp Business → tap your Broadcast List → tap 📎 (attach) → tap Gallery → select your offer image → tap Send.

Everyone in the list receives it as a private message — not a group. They cannot see each other.

🔄

When new customers register — update the list

Come back here → Load / Refresh → Save Contacts to Phone (vCard) again → import (duplicates skipped automatically) → open WhatsApp Business → tap and hold your Broadcast List → tap Edit → add new contacts → save. Done.

⚠ Important: Customers must have YOUR number saved in their contacts for the broadcast to deliver to them. When they first register, send them one message so they save your number. After that all broadcasts deliver normally.
✅ 100% free · unlimited contacts · no third-party app · works on all Android phones
🍎 iPhone — WhatsApp Business Broadcast List (Free)
iPhone does not allow third-party apps to control WhatsApp. The best free method is WhatsApp Business broadcast lists. One-time setup, then sending takes 1 tap per list.
1

Install WhatsApp Business from App Store

App Store → search WhatsApp Business → Install (free). Set it up with your dealer WhatsApp number.

2

Save contacts to your phone (tap button above)

Tap "📇 Save Contacts to Phone (vCard)" above → a .vcf file downloads → tap it → tap "Add All Contacts". All customers save as "AKAI D001 – Name" in your iPhone contacts.

3

Create Broadcast List in WhatsApp Business

Open WhatsApp Business → tap Broadcast Lists (top of chats screen) → New List → search "AKAI D001" → all your customers appear → select all → tap Create. Name it "My Customers". Make one list per 256 contacts.

4

Send image to everyone — 1 tap per list

Open WhatsApp Business → tap your Broadcast List → tap 📎 → choose your offer image → tap Send. Everyone receives it as a private message instantly.

🔄

When new contacts register

Come back here → Load / Refresh → Save Contacts to Phone → import vCard again (duplicates skipped automatically) → open WhatsApp Business → edit broadcast list → add new names. Takes 2 minutes.

⚠ Important for iPhone broadcast lists: Customers must have YOUR number saved in their contacts for the broadcast to deliver. When they register via QR, send them one WhatsApp message asking them to save your number. After that, all broadcasts deliver normally.
✅ 100% free · works on iPhone · no third-party app needed · just WhatsApp Business
